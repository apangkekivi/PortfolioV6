    About Me

    Greetings! I'm Pangjong, but you can call me Apang. Hailing from Nagaland, India, I've carved my path as a self-taught developer. Though my journey officially began in 2020, I found myself often pulled in different directions by life's demands, hindering my full immersion into the world of coding. Despite graduating with a degree in B.A., far removed from the tech realm, my passion for programming has always burned brightly within me.

    In February 2023, I made a pivotal decision to bid farewell to my 9-5 job, dedicating every waking moment to mastering the craft of programming. Initially diving into Python for eight months, I encountered challenges and setbacks, yet my determination never wavered. Recognizing the need for a fresh approach, I pivoted towards HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

    Today, armed with newfound confidence, I can proudly craft sleek, professional websites. As I embark on this exciting new chapter, I'm eager to continue evolving and embracing the endless possibilities that lie ahead.
